Model Overview & Evaluation

01 — Framework
AutoGluon TimeSeries

All forecasting models were trained using AutoGluon-TimeSeries, an open-source AutoML framework by Amazon that automates feature engineering, model selection, hyperparameter tuning, and ensemble construction for time series data.

We targeted daily log-returns of each equity over a 5-step forecast horizon (5 trading days). AutoGluon's TimeSeriesPredictor was configured to optimise for RMSE, with probabilistic forecasting enabled to produce full quantile distributions (Q0.05 – Q0.95) alongside point estimates.
